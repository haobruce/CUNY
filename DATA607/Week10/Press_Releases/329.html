Announcement

2010: Queen's Birthday Honours for Service personnel and Defence civilians

From:
Ministry of Defence
First published:
12 June 2010

This news article was published under the 2010 to 2015 Conservative and Liberal Democrat coalition government

141 UK Service personnel have been granted state honours by Her Majesty The Queen in her annual Birthday Honours list.

44 civilians have also been honoured either for work in the Ministry of Defence or in other aspects of UK Defence.

The full Defence Birthday Honours list for 2010 is below:
